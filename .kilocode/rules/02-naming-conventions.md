# Naming Conventions

- Classes & types: `CamelCase` (e.g., `UserProfile`).
- Functions & variables: `snake_case` (e.g., `process_data`).
- Constants: `SCREAMING_SNAKE_CASE` (e.g., `MAX_BUFFER_SIZE`).
- Modules & packages: `snake_case`.
- Private members: leading `_`.
- Files & folders: ASCII, no spaces.
